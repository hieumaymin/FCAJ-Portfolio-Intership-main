**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 2 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [AWS Foundations, Networking, Security, and Hands-on Labs](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [Hands-on with AWS Compute (EC2, Auto Scaling, Load Balancing), Monitoring (CloudWatch), Backup, and Storage Services](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [AWS fundamentals learning, and SpendWise project planning.](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [Infrastructure setup using Terraform and AWS networking configuration.](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [Deploy RDS database and implement secrets management and HTTPS.](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Set up monitoring, logging, and cost control mechanisms.](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Optimize performance, implement auto-scaling, and reduce costs.](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Finalize documentation, validate DR, and complete project delivery.](1.8-week8/)
